Problem Statement

HORYZN’s Mission Pulse aims to build an emergency unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) to deliver a defibrillator. In Germany, 75,000 patients experience sudden cardiac arrest every year and only 11 % survive. The survival rate can be tripled, by shortening the ambulance‘s response time from 9 to 4 minutes. Since the UAV will serve populated areas, flight safety plays a fundamental role and can be enhanced by developing an AI using aerial imagery.
